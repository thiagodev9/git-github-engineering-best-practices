# Engineering Metrics / Métricas de Engenharia

Engineering metrics help teams measure delivery performance and reliability.
Métricas de engenharia ajudam equipes a medir desempenho de entrega e confiabilidade.

## Key metrics / Métricas chave

- Lead time: time from idea to production.
- Deployment frequency: how often code is released.
- Change failure rate: percent of deployments that fail.
- MTTR (Mean Time to Recovery): how quickly the system recovers.

## Why it matters / Por que importa

These metrics are part of the DORA mindset and help teams improve continuous delivery, stability, and operational excellence.
Essas métricas fazem parte do mindset DORA e ajudam equipes a melhorar entrega contínua, estabilidade e excelência operacional.
